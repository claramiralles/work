About me.

Creativity and learning new concepts are two of my greatest passions.

After being specialised in Product Marketing, at the beginning of 2019 I made a step forward towards a career in front-end development, thanks to winning a scholarship at ADALAB.

ADALAB is a 4-months-highly intense bootcamp targeted to include women into the tech market.

Here I was trained to be a fast learner and quickly adapt to work in different teams. Nowadays, what I enjoy the most as a developer is being able to convert people ideas into a reality.
